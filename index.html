One-Click Democracy

OCD is a social media platform focusing on empowering democracy, involving the people in public issues, and overseeing the representatives and government by directly reaching or reminding them through the features of the app.
2

Mission Statement

To make people’s voices heard by the governments and policymakers. We believe that positive changes can be realized in this world by listening to and considering people’s aspirations. With this platform, we aim to encourage the establishment of a smart and open government system and also smart people in Indonesia.
